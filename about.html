About BoilerService.com

BoilerService.com is a microsite created to provide practical information and resources around boiler servicing, maintenance, and heating system care — for homeowners, landlords, and heating professionals alike.

Whether you need to understand what an annual service involves, troubleshoot a fault code, or find technical documentation for your boiler, we aim to point you in the right direction quickly.

This site is part of the MyBoiler.com family of resources. MyBoiler.com offers a wider range of guides covering energy saving, combi boilers, heat pumps, fault diagnostics, and more.

For free boiler manuals — including installation, service, and user guides — visit our sister site BoilerManuals.com, which hosts literature for dozens of boiler and control manufacturers.

Related sites

  • MyBoiler.com — Smarter heating & hot water resources
  • BoilerManuals.com — Free boiler and controls manuals

Our boiler service checklist provides step-by-step servicing guidance for heating professionals, including condensing and non-condensing boiler checks and FGA emissions ratios — previously hosted on Hub.MyBoiler.com.

We are building out additional features — including tools to help you find and compare qualified heating engineers in your area. Check back soon, or get in touch via MyBoiler.com if you have questions or suggestions.
